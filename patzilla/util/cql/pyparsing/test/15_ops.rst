Keyword extraction
------------------

>>> CQL('pa all "central, intelligence, agency" and US').polish().keywords()
['central', 'intelligence', 'agency']

>>> CQL('pa all "central intelligence agency" and US').polish().keywords()
['central', 'intelligence', 'agency']
